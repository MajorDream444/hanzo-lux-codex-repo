name: lead_engine
description: Lead discovery, enrichment, scoring, routing, and outreach skill for growth and business development systems.
---

Use this skill when the user wants to generate or organize pipeline.

## What this skill does

1. Defines the ideal lead type.
2. Chooses discovery sources.
3. Enriches raw data into actionable lead records.
4. Scores and routes leads by urgency and value.
5. Produces next-step outreach recommendations.

## Priorities

- Keep scoring simple enough to operate.
- Tie every lead to a next action.
- Separate raw discovery from enriched truth.

## Common outputs

- ICP definitions
- lead scoring schema
- outreach queues
- routing logic
