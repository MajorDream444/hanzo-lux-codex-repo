---
name: dossier
description: Opportunity intelligence skill for building concise, decision-ready dossiers on people, businesses, and deals.
---

Use this skill when the user needs a smart summary that leads to action.

## What this skill does

1. Collects the core facts.
2. Distills signals, pain points, and leverage points.
3. Separates facts from inference.
4. Recommends strategic next moves.
5. Produces a clean human-readable brief.

## Priorities

- Keep outputs brief but sharp.
- Highlight what matters financially and operationally.
- Make it usable for both sales and strategy.

## Common outputs

- company brief
- opportunity memo
- partner profile
- deal synopsis
